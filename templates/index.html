After Action Reports?

After Action Reports, or AARs, are a form of community engagement for Megagames. After playing in a game, most organizers will invite players to put together an AAR, describing their game experience. These are then shared between players and other megagame enthusiasts.

Why Create This Site?

Traditionally players post their AARs in a variety of places

  • Reddit
  • Facebook Groups
  • Personal Blogs
  • Other generally disparate locations on the internet

This is non-optimal for a variety of reasons, the biggest one being that they fracture the community. Given that megagames are already a fairly niche topic, a lack of centralized resources makes it hard for new community members to get involved.

Getting Involved

After the Action is still in active development, and not ready to go live. If you're interested in getting involved, email me at don@kuntz.co.

Development is done in the open, and the eventual source that will be run here can be found on GitHub. Contributions are more than welcome.
